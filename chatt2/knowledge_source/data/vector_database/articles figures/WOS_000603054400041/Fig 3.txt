Figure 3.   Effect of β-rubromycin on infection and in planta growth of Ph. infestans. (A) Tomato leaflets  inoculated with zoospores with or without 1.0 mg/L β-rubromycin. The image was taken after 9 days. (B)  Leaflets infected with a sporangium suspension with or without 1.0 mg/L β-rubromycin that was incubated at  25 °C for 8 h to stimulate direct germination. The image was taken after 9 days. Representative images for three  biological replicates are presented.
